How the Signal Engine Works
This terminal generates signals using a strict, rule-based liquidity sweep strategy. No indicators are used — only raw price structure and key levels. All 7 conditions must be confirmed before any signal is generated.
Timeframes Used
M5
All signal conditions — liquidity grab, strong move, and entry — are identified on the 5-minute chart.
M15
Trend shift confirmation uses the 15-minute chart. A confirmed trend shift on M5 is required; visibility on M15 increases the confidence score.
Key Levels Monitored
PDH/L
Prior Day High & Low — yesterday's highest and lowest traded prices. Frequently swept before major moves.
ASH/L
Asia Session High & Low — the range formed during the overnight Asian session (00:00–08:00 UTC). London and New York sessions often target these levels first.
EQH/L
Equal Highs / Equal Lows — two or more candles touching the same price level within 0.05%. These clusters signal where stop orders are likely sitting.
What Counts as a Valid Liquidity Grab
BUY
Candle low breaks below level by ≥ 0.02% AND closes back above level. Lower wick ≥ 30% of total range.
SELL
Candle high breaks above level by ≥ 0.02% AND closes back below level. Upper wick ≥ 30% of total range.
What Counts as a Strong Move (Displacement)
01
Body size ≥ 1.5× average of last 10 candle bodies before sweep.
02
BUY: closes in top 25% of candle range. SELL: closes in bottom 25%.
03
Must occur within 1–3 candles after sweep. If not found in 3 bars → setup invalidated.
What Counts as a Trend Shift (Break of Structure)
BUY
Identify last lower-high in lookback. Break above by ≥ 0.05% on close = BOS confirmed.
SELL
Identify last higher-low in lookback. Break below by ≥ 0.05% on close = BOS confirmed.
M15
BOS must also be visible on M15 chart (price closes beyond BOS level on M15 timeframe).
When Does the Entry Trigger
Zone
50%–61.8% Fibonacci retracement of displacement range (sweep low/high → impulse extreme).
Confirm
Price must enter zone AND print a rejection candle (bullish close + lower wick for BUY; bearish close + upper wick for SELL).
Reject
Pullback exceeds 70% retracement → setup invalidated. Immediate continuation (no pullback) → wait.
Stop Loss & Take Profit Placement
SL: 1–2 pips beyond sweep wick OR 0.1 × ATR(14) buffer, whichever is larger.
TP1: nearest opposing liquidity level with minimum 1.5R distance. TP2: next level or 3.5R fallback. Minimum TP1 R:R = 1:2 required or setup rejected.
How Confidence is Calculated
+20
Session valid (London or New York active)
+20
Clean sweep confirmed (0.02% break + wick ≥ 30%)
+20
Strong displacement (1.5× body, close at extreme)
+20
Clear BOS on M5 (+ M15 visible)
+20
Clean pullback into 50–61.8% zone with rejection candle
A minimum score of 80 out of 100 is required before a signal is generated.
Time Limit on Setups
Once a liquidity grab is detected, the full sequence — strong move, trend shift, and pullback entry — must complete within 10 candles (50 minutes). If it takes longer, the setup is marked invalid and the engine resets.
When Signals Are Suppressed (Volatility)
The current volatility (ATR) must be within a valid range for Gold and Silver. If the market is too quiet or unusually chaotic (e.g. news spike), signals are suppressed until conditions normalise.
